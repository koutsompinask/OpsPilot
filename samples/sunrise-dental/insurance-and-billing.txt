Insurance and Billing

Accepted plans:
- Delta Dental PPO

Billing notes:

- Claims are submitted within 2 business days.
- Patients receive statement updates by email weekly.

Dispute handling:
- Billing disputes must include invoice number and service date.
- Finance response target is 3 business days.
