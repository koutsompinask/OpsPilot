# Appointment Policy

## Scheduling
- New patient appointments require phone verification.
- Online booking confirmations are sent within 5 minutes.

## Late Arrival
- Arrivals over 15 minutes late may require rescheduling.
- Emergency cases are prioritized by triage nurse.

## Cancellation
- Cancellations within 24 hours may incur a late fee.
- Same-day cancellations are reviewed case by case.
