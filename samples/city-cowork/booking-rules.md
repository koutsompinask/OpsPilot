# Booking Rules

## Meeting Rooms
- Standard slots are 30 or 60 minutes.
- Maximum booking horizon is 14 days.
- No-show after 10 minutes releases the room automatically.

## Quiet Zones
- Calls are not permitted in quiet zones.
- Repeated noise violations trigger access suspension.

## Guest Policy
- Members can invite up to 2 guests per day.
- Guests must sign in at reception.
