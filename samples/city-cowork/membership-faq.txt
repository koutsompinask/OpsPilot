Membership FAQ

Q: What are support desk hours?
A: Monday-Friday, 08:00 to 20:00.

Q: Can I pause my membership?
A: Yes, once per 6-month period for up to 30 days.

Q: How do I report Wi-Fi issues?
A: Open a support ticket at front desk or email support@citycowork.example.

Q: Are printers included?
A: Black-and-white printing is included up to 200 pages monthly.
