# Housekeeping Standards

## Daily Service Window
- Standard room service window: **09:00-16:00**.
- Do-not-disturb rooms are skipped and retried once at 17:30.

## Linen Replacement
- Towels replaced daily unless guest opts out.
- Bed linen replaced every 3 nights for long stays.

## Escalation Rules
- Report maintenance issues in ticketing system within 10 minutes.
- Mark room as out-of-service if issue is safety related.
- Notify floor supervisor for any guest complaint rated high priority.
