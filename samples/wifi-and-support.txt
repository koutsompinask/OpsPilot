Wi-Fi and Technical Support Guide

Primary network: HarborGuest
Fallback network: HarborGuest-Backup

Standard login procedure:
1. Connect to HarborGuest.
2. Open browser and complete captive portal form.
3. Enter room number and last name.

If guest cannot connect:
- Ask guest to forget network and reconnect.
- Reboot guest device.
- Verify MAC randomization is disabled if portal fails.
- Escalate to IT hotline extension 440 if unresolved after 10 minutes.

Support desk hours: 07:00-23:00 daily.
