Refund and Cancellation Policy

Flexible rate:
- Free cancellation up to 24 hours before arrival.
- Within 24 hours, first night is non-refundable.

Non-refundable rate:
- Full stay is charged at booking.
- No refunds for cancellation or no-show.

Billing disputes:
- Customer must submit dispute within 14 days of checkout.
- Provide reservation code, receipt, and reason for dispute.
- Finance team response SLA: 3 business days.
