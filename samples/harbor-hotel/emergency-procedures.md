# Emergency Procedures

## Fire Alarm
1. Instruct guests to leave belongings behind.
2. Direct evacuation to Assembly Point A (main parking lot).
3. Front desk prints occupancy list for emergency responders.

## Medical Incident
1. Call local emergency number immediately.
2. Notify duty manager on extension 101.
3. Record incident details in the Safety Log within 15 minutes.

## Power Outage
- Backup generators restore critical systems within 60 seconds.
- Elevators may be unavailable for up to 5 minutes.
- Offer flashlights from front desk stock cabinet B.
