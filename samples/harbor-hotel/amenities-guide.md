# Amenities Guide

## Breakfast
- Served daily from **07:00 to 10:30** in the Garden Hall.
- Vegan and gluten-free options available on request.

## Gym
- Open **24/7** with room key access.
- Guests must wear athletic shoes and carry a towel.

## Pool
- Open from **08:00 to 21:00**.
- Children under 12 must be supervised by an adult.

## Spa
- Appointments required.
- Last booking slot starts at **20:00**.
